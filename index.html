About Me

Leading with technology, delivering with purpose

I am a dynamic professional with over 10 years of experience in team management, productivity enhancement, leadership, project coordination and organizational development. I currently serve as Assistant General Manager (AGM), Productivity Specialist, Entrepreneur, Professional Speaker and Facilitator at S M Techno Consultants Pvt. Ltd., where I actively contribute to business growth, team development and the successful execution of corporate and government projects.

Throughout my professional journey I have worked with various corporate organizations and government clients, handling responsibilities across strategic planning, process improvement, team management and productivity enhancement.

During my career I have had the privilege of working under the guidance of several respected leaders and senior government officials, and collaborating with senior officers across the police, administrative and forest services, as well as international parliamentary and ministerial leaders.

“I continue to create meaningful impact by empowering teams, improving operational efficiency and driving innovation in professional environments.”

With strong expertise in leadership, productivity management, corporate training, government project coordination, business strategy and team motivation, I bring together hands-on technology depth and an executive perspective.
